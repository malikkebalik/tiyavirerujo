    Dave Annable is an American actor who has a net worth of $3 million. Dave Annable is best known for his portrayal as Justin Walker on the television drama series "Brothers & Sisters," which ran from 2006 to 2011.

    Early Life

    David Annable was born on September 15, 1979 in Suffem, New York. He was raised in the small town of Walden. Growing up he was active in sports, playing baseball, rugby and hockey. He graduated from Valley Central High School in 1997.

    He became involved with the student-ran TV station Plattsburgh State Television (PSTV) while attending the State University of New York at Plattsburgh (SUNY Plattsburgh). At PSTV Annable contributed to both the production behind the scenes as well as on air. On the station he hosted "Late Night with Dave Annable," "Cardinal Sports," and "The Roommate Game."

    Career

    After he left SUNY Plattsburgh, Dave took lessons at the Neighborhood Playhouse in New York. His acting debut was on an episode of the NBC Crime Drama television series "Third Watch" in 2002. Around this time he appeared in several television commercials for such popular products as, Mountain Dew, Starburst and Abercrombie and Finch. In 2004 he roles in the films "Little Black Book "and "Spellbound."

    Brothers & Sisters

    Annable's big break came in 2006 when he was cast as Justin Walker in the hit television series Brothers & Sisters. The show aired on ABC and ran for five seasons, with Annable's performance earning him widespread acclaim and recognition. He was nominated for a Prism Award for his portrayal of the character, which explored topics such as addiction, homosexuality, and family dynamics.

    Kevin Winter/Getty Images

    Other Work

    Aside from his role in Brothers & Sisters, Annable has appeared in a variety of other television series and films. He played Dr. Adam McAndrew in the Fox medical drama Red Band Society and Henry Martin in the supernatural series Midnight, Texas. In film, he has had roles in Little Black Book, What's Your Number?, and The Truth About Lies.

    Annable has also worked as a voice actor, lending his voice to several video games and animated shows, including the popular video game Call of Duty: Black Ops 2 and the animated series Wolverine and the X-Men.

    Personal Life

    David Annable married actress Odette Yustman in 2010, and the couple has two children together. They often post photos of their family on social media, giving fans a glimpse into their personal life. Annable is also actively involved in charity work, supporting organizations such as St. Jude Children's Research Hospital and the Elizabeth Glaser Pediatric AIDS Foundation.

    Real Estate

    In May 2008 David paid $1.5 million for a home in Los Angeles. He and Odette listed the home for sale in November 2019 for $2.65 million, accepting $2.43 million in April 2020. Upon selling their LA home, the family moved to Austin, Texas.
