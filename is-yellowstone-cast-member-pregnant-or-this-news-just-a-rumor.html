Since Kevin Costner’s wife filed for divorce, he has become the subject of unfavourable rumours, including one that claims the actor was responsible for the pregnancy of a “Yellowstone” set worker. However, this is a claim and the news is completely untrue, according to sources.

The most recent rumour circulating online about Costner is “total BS” according to sources close to the actor. It’s unclear who started the rumour or even which Yellowstone Cast Member Pregnant. It has been going viral on social media since his wife, Christine announced her intention to file for divorce on Monday, May 1, 2023.

One Twitter user wrote:

“I wonder now if the controversy surrounding Kevin Costner and the Yellowstone movie’s “schedule” was brought on by the claim that he might have become pregnant on the set.”

Another Twitter added:

“So Costner, rather than Bentley was responsible for a girl becoming pregnant on the Yellowstone set.”

The actor’s representative made it obvious that Costner was taken off guard by Christine’s petition when they said:

“Circumstances beyond his control have transpired which have resulted in Mr. Costner having to participate in a dissolution of marriage action.”

Kevin was unaware that Christine was seeking to dissolve their 18-year marriage and is still unaware of the situation. Since they were married in 2004, Christine specifies the date of separation in her divorce documents as last month.

Interesting given that reports claim Kevin was unaware the marriage was ending. The prenup stipulates that Christine must leave Kevin’s three houses and Costner referred to it in his response to the divorce suit. Kevin and Christine both want shared custody of their three children.
