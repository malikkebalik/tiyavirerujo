Terry Farrell is an American actress. Her career originally started in the mid-1980s. She made her debut with a starring role in the television series Paper Dolls. She later made her first movie role in Back to School. At the same time, she also acted in the TV films Beverly Hills Madam and The Deliberate Stranger. She continued acting in the ’90s, first appearing in the movie Hellraiser III: Hell on Earth. From 1993 to 1998, she became widely known for starring as Jadzia Dax in the series Star Trek: Deep Space Nine. After this ended, she starred as Regina “Reggie” Kostas in Becker.
