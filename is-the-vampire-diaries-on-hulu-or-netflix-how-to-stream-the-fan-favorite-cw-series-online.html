Is 'The Vampire Diaries' on Netflix or Hulu? Find out where to stream the fan favorite CW series online as well as how successful the show was.

Published on October 5, 2019

2 min read

The Vampire Diaries gave fans all the vampire goodness — or badness— they could handle. For 8 years, fans followed the adventures of the Salvatore brothers and Elena Gilbert, as she tried to choose between them. Yet all good things must come to an end. This show came to an end in 2017.

Fortunately, fans can always rewatch their favorite shows. So if you’re a fan, or are simply intrigued by the show, and are looking to watch it, you’ve come to the right place. Without any further ado, let’s get into it.

Is ‘The Vampire Diaries’ on Hulu or Netflix?

Are you ready to find out where you can watch The Vampire Diaries? Are you sure? Ok. It’s available to stream, all 8 wonderful seasons of it, on Netflix.

It is not available to stream on Hulu, nor does it seem likely that it ever will be. However, you never know. Perhaps one day it will pop up on the streaming service.

Where else can you stream ‘The Vampire Diaries’?

If you don’t have Netflix, there’s no problem. The Vampire Diaries is available to purchase on Amazon for $1.99 per episode or $24.99 for seasons 1-3 and $29.99 for seasons 4-8. The series is also available to purchase on Youtube.

However, if you don’t want to spend extra money in order to watch the series, you’re out of luck. The show can not be streamed for free anywhere. Therefore the best option, without spending any extra money, is probably Netflix. Though we’ll let you decide for yourself if it’s worth it or not to spend the extra money, since the show is a good one.

How successful was ‘The Vampire Diaries’?

It’s safe to say that the show was a success. It was a hit for the CW, and therefore ran for 8 seasons. Even in its last season, it got good ratings, ending on a high of 1.148 million viewers.

Though that was actually not an all-time high for The Vampire Diaries, which averaged 3.53 million total viewers in season 7. So the ratings did go down a little for the final season. Yet the show was still viewed by the CW as a success and it was certainly very popular for them.

The acting on the show is superb. The writing can be difficult at times, but overall it’s good. Anyone watching — or rewatching — the show is in for a real supernatural treat!

Once you see the show, you will be drawn in and won’t be able to stop watching. So good thing it’s on Netflix, because it’s a great show to binge-watch. This is a must-see show, not only because it’s popular, but also because it’s always exciting and keeps you on the edge of your seat.

Nina Dobrev as Elena Gilbert makes watching the show worthwhile just by being a fantastic actress portraying a wonderful character. Ian Somerhalder as Damon Salvatore is also amazing, as is Paul Wesley as Stefan Salvatore. So let’s all go watch The Vampire Diaries now.
