When Did Lacey Chabert’s Debut as an Actress?

On September 30, 1982, Lacey Nicole Chabert was born in Purvis, Mississippi. Her father is a Louisiana native of Cajun. She has an elder sister named Chrissy and a younger brother named Tony. In November 2021, Wendy, their eldest sibling, passed away from a heart attack.

In the 1985 “World’s Our Little Miss Scholarship Competition,” she won the title of “World’s Baby Petite.” When she was younger, she competed in beauty pageants and won the title of Miss Jr. Mississippi.

Playing Erica Kane’s daughter Bianca Montgomery on “All My Children” from 1992 to 1993 was one of her first appearances. After that, she became even more well-known as a young actor when she played Claudia Salinger in the Fox series Party of Five.

In addition to two feature films, Rugrats Go Wild and The Wild Thornberrys Movie, Chabert voiced Eliza Thornberry in the Nickelodeon animated series The Wild Thornberrys.
