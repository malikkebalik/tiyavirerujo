What is Jessy Terrero's Net Worth?

Jessy Terrero is a Dominican film director and music video director who has a net worth of $6 million. Jessy Terrero was born in the Dominican Republic in October 1972. He has directed the films Buscando un sueno in 1997, Better Living in 1998, Soul Plane in 2004, The Confession in 2006, Brooklyn to Manhattan and Gun in 2010, and Freelancers in 2012.

Terrero has also directed several music videos for a variety of artists including Sean Paul, Jill Scott, Mystikal, Petey Pablo, Lil' Romeo, 50 Cent, Styles P, Chingy, G-Unit, Lloyd Banks, Terror Squad, Young Jeezy, Unwritten Law, Chris Brown, Mobb Deep, Wilsin & Yandel, Ludacris, Danity Kane, Enrique Iglesias, Future, Jennifer Lopez, Juanes, Ricky Martin, and more. Terrero has been nominated for a MTV Video Music Award, M2 Award, and Billboard Music Video Award for Jill Scott's "Getting' In The Way". He has also been nominated for a Billboard Music Video Award for Syleena Johnson's "I Am Your Woman" and an MTV Video Music Award for 50 Cent's "Candy Shop".
